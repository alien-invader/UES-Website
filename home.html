Undergraduate Economics Society

UCSD's Largest Economics Organization

What We Offer

UES is the official student organization for the Department of Economics at UCSD, where we are dedicated to creating a community for students majoring in or interested in economics.

Professional Development

Attend our workshops to prepare yourself for your career! We host interview, resume, and career info workshops hosted by professional guest speakers.


Personal Growth

Discover and grow your values through connecting with other UES members and alumni! On top of connecting with fellow undergraduates, we also host mixers with alumni and graduate students who are eager to help undergraduates.


Community

Have fun with us through our socials! Join us when we eat food, go ice-skating, or have study jams at Geisel!
